My Personal Interests

I generally enjoy learning and messing around with electronics. A couple activities include creating HDL for FPGA via System Verilog, learning about Linux through Arch as well as embedded SoC devices, electronic repair and modifications, homebrewing video game consoles, and circuit design. Currently, I'm trying to use electronics to demonstrate principles from physics.

I enjoy playing a variety of video games. I generally play older games from the Xbox 360, wii, Playstation 2, Nintetndo 64, and Super Nintendo. A couple of my favorite games are Fallout New Vegas, Sly Cooper Series, Burnout 3, 007 Nightfire, Mario Kart Wii, Super Mario 64, Donkey Kong 64, and Super Mario World. I also really enjoy playing rhythm games such as Dance Dance Revolution and Beat Saber.

I like designing 3D models and understanding how to print them. I use both a Prusa MK4S and a Bamboo X1 Carbon depending on the project. Currently, I'm printing cabinets or "cubbies" to hold and organize my small electronic components. I've also printed a camera mount to mount my remote camera to my guinea pig cage and a keychain of the Wasatch Ranger while I was the computer teacher at Wasatch Elementary.
